Our Mission

When a doctor appointment can be hard to get, we are there to help people with Chronic Kidney Disease have access to the dietary tools neccessary to prevent a worsening condition.


Kidney is designed to be your personal dietary coach when your doctor can't be there. It can help you know exactly what to include in your diet to stay healthy and prevent worsening CKD symptoms. Simply create an account and input some information for us so we can calculate your personal daily recommended micronutrient, macronutrient and water amounts.
