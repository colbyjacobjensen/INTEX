Our Mission

We are there for people suffering from Chronic Kidney Disease when a doctor can't be. We seek to provide access to neccessary dietary tools help prevent worsening CKD symptoms and live a healthier lifestyle.


Kidney is designed to be your personal dietary coach. It can help you know exactly what to include in your diet to stay healthy and prevent worsening CKD symptoms. We designed an interface that is personal to YOU. It considers your condition, gender, and weight to provide the most accurate suggestions for your diet. Simply create an account and input some information to get Started!
